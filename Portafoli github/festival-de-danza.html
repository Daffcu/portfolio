For this project, what has been entrusted is the development of the design to represent and disseminate the international dance festival of Barcelona, which is held on June 10, 11 and 12. The conceptualization for this design has been elaborated according to a game of forms that represent the movement of the dancers on stage. The idea of the poster is that an organic movement is represented through a static image.
